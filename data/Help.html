PathX

PathX is a casual game where one tries to live a life of crime. However, unlike a typical life of crime, you steal money from immoral people. The player traverses a map and tries to complete every level such that they attain the greatest amount of money possible. However, their journey will not be an easy one as cops, bandits and zombies will make this a difficult feat as they try to steal the money you have away. Additionally, you have to try and obey traffic laws (because driving is dangerous and apparently you have a conscience) which makes for a rather challenging time.

This game was developed in conjuction by Junior Computer Science Major Zeb Aslam and several thousand lines of code provided by Professor Richard Mckenna of Stony Brook University. Zeb is a television enthusiast and a foodie and enjoys Beyonce.
